About the Program

RoboticsForYouth.ca is a youth-led initiative dedicated to expanding access to STEM education for students in our local community. Through engaging workshops and real-world projects, we empower youth to explore coding, robotics, and engineering in a collaborative space.

Led by Manas Pandey, a high school student passionate about education, the program fosters creativity and leadership while bridging opportunity gaps for underrepresented youth.
