About the Program

This summer, join us for an exciting robotics and coding program designed specifically for youth in our ethnic community. Our goal is to provide hands-on experience in robotics and computer programming, build leadership and teamwork skills, and spark a lifelong interest in STEM fields.

How It Works

The program runs for two weeks in July and includes both in-person workshops and virtual mentoring. Participants will learn:

  • Basics of programming using Python
  • How robots work with real sensors and motors
  • How to build and program simple robotic systems
  • Problem-solving and engineering design challenges
All materials will be provided, and no prior experience is needed!
